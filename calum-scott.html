Calum Scott's Journey

Calum Scott is a British singer-songwriter known for his emotive vocals and heartfelt lyrics. He gained widespread recognition with his rendition of Robyn's "Dancing On My Own," which became a global hit. Since then, he has released several successful singles and albums, including "You Are The Reason," "Rhythm Inside," and the album "Bridges."

Calum's Fame Song

"You Are the Reason" is a song by Calum Scott, released in 2017 as part of his debut album Only Human (2018). The song is a heartfelt ballad about love, regret, and the longing to fix past mistakes. The lyrics express deep emotions, with the singer acknowledging his faults and wishing to make things right with someone special.
